## Project Description
This project focuses on analyzing an HR dataset to understand employee attrition and identify key factors affecting workforce dynamics.  
An interactive Tableau dashboard was developed to visualize trends related to employee demographics, job satisfaction, overtime, marital status, and career progression.  
The goal is to provide actionable insights to support data-driven HR decision-making.

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains employee-level HR information including:
- Demographics (age group, gender, marital status)
- Job-related attributes (department, job role, overtime)
- Satisfaction metrics (job satisfaction, work-life balance)
- Career timeline variables (years at company, total working years)
- Attrition status (Yes/No)

Temporal analysis is performed using career timeline variables such as years at company and total working years.
